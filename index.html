Density of common suffixes in populated areas of Antarctica

A visual scherzo inspired by Moritz Stefaner’s -ach, -ingen, -zell and Mahir Yavuz’s U.S. Suffix Density visualizations, both of which are great and far less silly than this one. Darker circles represent higher relative density of that suffix in that area relative to other areas of Antarctica.

Shape data from the Natural Earth project. Antarctic city data from GeoNames. Code available on GitHub

Evan Sheehan 2016.
